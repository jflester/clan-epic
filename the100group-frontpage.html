EPIC MEMBERSHIP

CURRENT MEMBERS
If you are a member of the EPIC the100 group, and want that sweet EPIC tag on your in-game emblem:
1) Join the main EPIC Bungie group here
2) As the EPIC clan within that group is full, also join this group
3) Once group join is approved, click the 'Set as Xbox Clan' button on that same group

WANT TO JOIN EPIC?
- Light level 200 or 400. First time raider or expert raid sherpa. .5 k/d or 3.0 k/d. Thats not important! What matters is you are friendly, mature, and respectful. If you are that, then you you belong with our family!
- To join us, click the "Join Group" button on this page, then join this game
- If one of our members knows you, please mention their name(s) in the chat.
- If not, just say "hi" and one of our administrators will message you back in that chat, usually within a few hours, to set up a time for a quick party chat.
